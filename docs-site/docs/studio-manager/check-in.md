# Client Check-In

This page explains the three ways to check in a client when they arrive for a class.

**Navigation — Check-In:**
This feature is accessed from the **Calendar** (IT: *Calendario* · FR: *Calendrier* · DE: *Kalender* · ES: *Calendario* · PT: *Calendário* · NL: *Kalender* · PL: *Kalendarz* · TR: *Takvim*).

---

## Before you start

A client can only check in if they have a **confirmed booking** (IT: *prenotazione confermata*) for that class. Without a booking, check-in will be rejected.

---

## The three check-in methods

### Method 1 — App check-in (client self-checks)

The client opens the Agon mobile app and taps **Check In** on their upcoming class. No action required from staff.

### Method 2 — QR code check-in

1. Go to **Calendar** (IT: *Calendario*) and open the class.
2. Click **Check-in mode**.
3. Your device's camera activates.
4. The client holds their phone QR code in front of the camera.
5. Check-in is confirmed automatically.

### Method 3 — Manual check-in

If a client doesn't have their phone:

1. Go to **Calendar** (IT: *Calendario*) and click the class.
2. In the detail panel, find the client.
3. Click **Check in** (IT: *Check-in*) next to their name.

## The check-in window

Check-in is only available within a time window around the class start:

- **Opens:** 30 minutes before class start (configurable in Settings)
- **Closes:** 15 minutes after class start (configurable in Settings)

Outside this window, app and QR check-in are rejected. **Manual check-in works at any time.**

You can adjust the check-in window in **Settings** (IT: *Impostazioni* · FR: *Paramètres* · DE: *Einstellungen* · ES: *Configuración* · PT: *Definições* · NL: *Instellingen* · PL: *Ustawienia* · TR: *Ayarlar*).

## What if something goes wrong?

**A client's app check-in is rejected**
Check that their booking status is **Confirmed** in the class panel. The check-in window may not have opened yet or may have already closed. Use manual check-in as a fallback.

**A client arrived but I forgot to check them in**
Use manual check-in from the class panel at any time — it works outside the check-in window.
