# Setting up the Agon client app

This page explains how your clients download and connect the Agon mobile app to your studio.

## What clients need

- An iPhone (iOS 15 or later) or Android phone (Android 8 or later)
- The QR code from your studio's onboarding sheet

## Steps for your clients

1. Download **Agon Studio** from the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play (Android).

2. Open the app. Tap **Scan studio QR code**.

3. Point the camera at the QR code printed at your reception desk.

4. The app connects to your studio automatically. The client creates their account with their name, email, and password.

5. Done — the client can now browse and book classes.

## If a client can't scan the QR code

Each QR code also has a short studio code printed below it. Clients can tap **Enter code manually** and type it in instead.
