# Notifications

Agon sends you push notifications to keep you informed about your bookings, classes, and membership. This page explains what notifications you'll receive and how to manage them.

---

## Types of notifications

| Notification | When you receive it |
|---|---|
| **Booking confirmed** | Immediately after you book a class |
| **Class reminder** | A set time before a class you're booked into (set by your studio, typically 2 hours before) |
| **Booking cancelled** | When you cancel a booking, or when the studio cancels a class you were booked into |
| **Waitlist offer** | When a spot opens in a full class you're on the waitlist for |
| **Waitlist confirmed** | When you confirm a waitlist spot and your booking is created |
| **Class cancelled** | When the studio cancels a class — all your credits are refunded automatically |
| **Membership expiring soon** | 7 days before your membership or credits expire |
| **Membership payment failed** | If a recurring payment for your subscription fails |

---

## Viewing your notification history

All notifications are saved in the app's notification inbox, even if you didn't see them when they arrived.

1. Open the **Agon Studio** app.
2. Tap the **bell icon** at the top of the screen.
3. Scroll through your notification history.

Tap any notification to go directly to the relevant booking or membership.

---

## Enabling or disabling notifications on iPhone (iOS)

1. Open your phone's **Settings** app.
2. Scroll down and tap **Agon Studio**.
3. Tap **Notifications**.
4. Toggle **Allow Notifications** on or off.

You can also choose whether notifications appear on the lock screen, in Notification Center, or as banners.

---

## Enabling or disabling notifications on Android

1. Open your phone's **Settings** app.
2. Tap **Apps** (or **Application Manager**, depending on your phone).
3. Find and tap **Agon Studio**.
4. Tap **Notifications**.
5. Toggle notifications on or off.

---

## What if something goes wrong?

**I'm not receiving notifications**
Make sure notifications are enabled for Agon Studio in your phone's Settings (see above). Also check that the app is connected to your studio — if your phone hasn't been able to reach the studio server recently, push tokens may be outdated. Try opening the app and checking your bookings; this refreshes the connection.

**I'm receiving too many notifications**
You can disable notifications entirely via your phone settings. Note that this also disables time-sensitive alerts like waitlist offers, which have a short confirmation window.

**I received a notification but tapping it doesn't open the right screen**
Try tapping **My Bookings** or **Membership** in the app to navigate manually.
