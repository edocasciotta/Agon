# Your membership

This page explains how memberships work in Agon, how to check your credits and expiry date, and how to purchase or pause a membership.

---

## Types of memberships

Your studio offers memberships in one of two forms:

**Recurring subscription**
You pay on a regular schedule (weekly, monthly, or annually). Each billing cycle, your credits are refreshed. Some subscriptions offer unlimited class access with no credit limit.

**Credit pack**
You pay once and receive a set number of credits that are valid for a fixed period (for example, 10 credits valid for 3 months). Once all credits are used, or the expiry date passes, whichever comes first, the membership is no longer active.

---

## Viewing your membership

1. Open the **Agon Studio** app.
2. Tap **Membership** at the bottom of the screen.

You'll see:
- Your **membership type** (e.g. "Monthly Unlimited", "10-Class Pack")
- Your **status** (Active, Paused, Expired)
- **Credits remaining** (if your membership uses credits)
- **Expiry date** (for credit packs, or the next renewal date for subscriptions)

---

## How credits work

Each time you book a class, **one credit** is deducted from your membership.

If you cancel a booking:
- **Cancelled early** (before the cancellation window): your credit is refunded
- **Cancelled late** (inside the cancellation window, e.g. within 2 hours of class start): depending on your studio's policy, you may or may not get the credit back. The app always tells you before you confirm a cancellation.

If you have a **recurring subscription** with credits, your credits reset when your subscription renews. Whether unused credits from the previous period carry over depends on your studio's settings for that membership type:

- If your studio has **credit rollover** enabled for your membership type, your unused credits carry over into the new period (up to a maximum set by your studio, if any) instead of being lost.
- If rollover is not enabled, unused credits from the previous period do not carry over — this is the default.

Check your membership details on the **Membership** screen, or ask your studio, to find out whether your plan includes rollover.

Some late cancellations or no-shows may also incur a fee set by your studio — the app always tells you before you confirm a cancellation whether a fee applies, and any fee charged will appear in your payment history.

## Purchasing a new membership

If your studio has enabled online purchases, you can buy a membership directly from the app:

1. Tap **Membership** in the app.
2. Tap **Browse memberships**.
3. Select the membership type you want.
4. Tap **Purchase**.
5. If you have a **promo code** and/or a **gift card**, you can enter them at this step — a promo code applies a percentage or fixed discount, and a gift card deducts from its remaining balance. Both can be applied to the same purchase, and the app shows you the final price before you confirm.
6. You're taken to a secure Stripe payment screen to enter your card details (unless a gift card already covers the full price, in which case your membership activates immediately with no card needed).
7. After payment, your membership is activated immediately.

If this is your **first purchase** of a membership type that has an **intro offer** configured, you'll automatically see a reduced intro price instead of the regular price — no code needed. Intro pricing only applies once per client across your studio's intro-offer membership types.

If online purchases are not available, contact your studio directly to purchase a membership.

---

## Pausing a membership

If your membership type allows pauses, and you need to take a break (for example, due to travel or injury):

1. Tap **Membership** in the app.
2. Tap **Request pause**.
3. Set the start and end dates for your pause.
4. Tap **Submit request**.

Your studio manager will review and approve or reject the pause. You'll receive a push notification with the result.

While your membership is paused:
- You cannot book classes
- Your expiry date is extended by the pause duration
- Billing is suspended for the pause period (for recurring subscriptions)

---

## What if something goes wrong?

**My membership shows as expired but I just paid**
If you paid via the app with Stripe, your membership should activate within a few seconds of payment. If it's still showing expired after a minute, try closing and reopening the app. If the problem continues, contact your studio — they can manually activate the membership.

**I have credits but the app won't let me book**
Make sure the class type is covered by your membership. Some memberships only apply to specific class types. Check the membership details on the **Membership** screen or ask your studio.

**I want to cancel my membership**
Contact your studio manager directly — membership cancellations are handled by the studio.
