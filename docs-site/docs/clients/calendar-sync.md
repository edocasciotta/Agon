# Calendar sync

This page explains how to subscribe your phone's calendar app to your Agon bookings, so upcoming classes show up automatically.

---

## Before you start

You need the **Agon Studio** app installed and to be logged in. Calendar sync works with any calendar app that supports subscribing to a feed URL — this includes the built-in Calendar app on iPhone and most Android calendar apps, as well as Google Calendar and Outlook.

---

## Subscribing to your calendar

1. Open the **Agon Studio** app.
2. Tap **Profile** at the bottom of the screen.
3. Find the **Calendar Sync** section.
4. Tap **Add to Calendar**.

This opens your device's calendar app and starts its native "subscribe to calendar" flow. Once you confirm there, your upcoming confirmed class bookings appear in your calendar automatically — and stay up to date as your bookings change, without you doing anything else.

### Copying the link instead

If you'd rather add the feed manually (for example, in a desktop calendar app), tap **Copy Link** on the same screen to copy your personal feed URL to your clipboard, then paste it into your calendar app's "subscribe by URL" option.

---

## Regenerating your link

Your calendar feed link is personal and secret — treat it like a password. If you think it's been shared or exposed, you can generate a new one:

1. On the **Calendar Sync** section of your **Profile** screen, tap **Regenerate**.
2. Confirm.

This immediately invalidates your old link. Any calendar app still using it will stop updating — you'll need to subscribe again using the new link (tap **Add to Calendar** or **Copy Link** again).

---

## What if something goes wrong?

**My classes aren't showing up in my calendar**
Most calendar apps only check a subscribed feed periodically (sometimes every few hours), not instantly. Try a manual refresh/sync in your calendar app, or wait a bit.

**Tapping "Add to Calendar" didn't open my calendar app**
Some calendar apps don't register as a handler for calendar subscription links. Try **Copy Link** instead and add it manually through your calendar app's "subscribe by URL" or "add calendar by URL" option.

**I regenerated my link and now nothing updates**
This is expected — regenerating invalidates the old link. Subscribe again with the new link shown after regenerating.
